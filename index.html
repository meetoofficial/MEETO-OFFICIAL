How MEETO Works

01

How does the planning process work?

The Planner selects friends, chooses the time, date, mood, and location radius. Everyone gets an invite, and once they accept, they swipe on suggested options and vote on the final choice.

02

⁠What happens after I accept a hangout invite?

You’ll start swiping on recommended spots based on the chosen mood. You get 3 likes to give to your favorite cards — the ones you swipe right on. Your votes help shape the group’s final decision.

03

How is the final plan decided?

After swiping, everyone participates in a final vote. The top-voted spot becomes the official hangout, no more endless group-chat debates.

04

What do I see in the event dashboard?

The dashboard gives you everything you need: final location, time, directions, booking links, who’s coming, and live locations so no one gets lost (yes, even your drunk friend).
