How MEETO Works

Three simple steps to amazing adventures

01

Plan the Hangout

The planner selects the friends, chooses the date, time, mood, and radius — then sends out the invite. Each friend can Accept or Deny with one tap.

02

⁠Swipe & Like Your Favorites

Once accepted, everyone swipes through suggested options. Each person gets 3 likes to give to the cards they swipe right on — choosing their top picks.

03

Vote, Confirm & Meet Up

The group enters a final vote to choose the winner. A shared dashboard instantly appears with directions, time, details, and who’s coming — so everyone shows up stress-free.
